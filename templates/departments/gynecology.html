Frequently Asked Questions

What is an annual gynecological exam?
An annual gynecological exam is a yearly check-up focusing on women's reproductive health. It typically includes a physical exam, breast exam, pelvic exam, and a Pap test (if needed) to screen for cervical cancer.
When should a young woman first see a gynecologist?
It's generally recommended for young women to have their first gynecological visit between the ages of 13 and 15. This initial visit often focuses on education, answering questions, and building a relationship with a healthcare provider, rather than a full pelvic exam.
What are common symptoms of menopause?
Common symptoms of menopause include hot flashes, night sweats, vaginal dryness, mood swings, sleep disturbances, and changes in menstrual cycles. These symptoms vary in intensity and duration among individuals.
