Frequently Asked Questions

What is mental health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.

When should I seek professional help?

If you're experiencing persistent sadness, anxiety, changes in sleep or appetite, difficulty concentrating, or thoughts of self-harm, it's advisable to seek professional help. Our team can provide a safe space for support.

What types of therapy do you offer?

We offer a range of therapies including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), psychodynamic therapy, family therapy, and group therapy, tailored to individual needs.

Is my information confidential?

Yes, patient confidentiality is a cornerstone of our practice. All your personal and medical information is kept strictly confidential in accordance with privacy laws and ethical guidelines.
